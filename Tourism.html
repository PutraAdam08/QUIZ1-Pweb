Kampung Batik Jetis

Kampung Batik Jetis in Sidoarjo is a vibrant and culturally rich village renowned for its traditional batik craftsmanship. Located in the heart of East Java, this charming community is steeped in the art of batik, a centuries-old Indonesian textile tradition. Visitors to Kampung Batik Jetis can immerse themselves in the intricate process of creating batik patterns, observing skilled artisans meticulously hand-painting designs on fabric using wax and dyes. The village exudes a quaint, rustic charm, with its narrow alleyways lined with colorful batik shops, allowing tourists to shop for exquisite batik garments and souvenirs. This cultural enclave provides a captivating glimpse into Indonesia's artistic heritage, making it a must-visit destination for those seeking a deeper connection with the country's rich traditions.
