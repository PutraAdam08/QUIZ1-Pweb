Lontong Kupang

Sidoarjo Lontong Kupang is a regional variation of this beloved Indonesian dish, hailing from the town of Sidoarjo in East Java. This particular rendition is celebrated for its unique twist on the classic Lontong Kupang. It features lontong, those cylindrical rice cakes that are the heart of the dish, cooked to a perfect chewy consistency. What sets Sidoarjo Lontong Kupang apart is its distinctive kupang sauce, which boasts a bold and robust flavor profile. The sauce, a fiery concoction of chili peppers, garlic, shallots, coconut milk, shrimp paste, and a blend of aromatic spices, packs a flavorful punch that's irresistible to spice enthusiasts. The use of Sidoarjo's local ingredients and culinary expertise creates a distinct regional flavor that makes Sidoarjo Lontong Kupang a must-try for anyone exploring the diverse world of Indonesian street food.
